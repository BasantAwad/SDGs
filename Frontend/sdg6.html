Information about SDG 6

SDG 6 aims to ensure access to clean water and sanitation for all. Despite progress, billions of people still lack access to safe drinking water and basic sanitation services. Achieving SDG 6 requires addressing various challenges such as:

  • Water scarcity and pollution
  • Lack of basic sanitation facilities
  • Unequal access to water and sanitation services
  • Water-related disasters and climate change impacts

These challenges need to be tackled through effective policies and interventions at local, national, and global levels.

Solutions and how you can help

There are several ways individuals can contribute to achieving SDG 6 and promoting clean water and sanitation:

  • Investing in water infrastructure and sanitation facilities
  • Promoting water conservation and efficient water use
  • Improving wastewater treatment and pollution control
  • Ensuring equal access to water and sanitation services for all
  • Building resilience to water-related disasters and climate change

By taking these actions, individuals can contribute to ensuring everyone has access to clean water and sanitation, which is essential for health and well-being.
