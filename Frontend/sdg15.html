Information about SDG 15

SDG 15 focuses on protecting, restoring, and promoting sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems. This goal calls for:

  • Halting deforestation and land degradation.
  • Conserving biodiversity and endangered species.
  • Promoting sustainable land management practices.

    • Taking action on SDG 15 involves reforestation efforts, wildlife conservation initiatives, and sustainable agriculture practices.

Solutions and how you can help

To support SDG 15 and promote sustainable land use, individuals and communities can:

  • Participate in reforestation and restoration projects to combat deforestation and land degradation.
  • Adopt sustainable agricultural practices that prioritize soil health and biodiversity conservation.
  • Advocate for policies that protect forests, wildlife habitats, and endangered species.
  • Educate others about the importance of conserving terrestrial ecosystems and biodiversity.

By implementing these solutions, we can preserve our natural landscapes and ensure the health of terrestrial ecosystems.
