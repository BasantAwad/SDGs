Information about SDG 16

SDG 16 aims to promote peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development. This goal calls for:

  • Promoting rule of law and access to justice for all.
  • Reducing violence and corruption.
  • Building effective, accountable, and transparent institutions.

    • Taking action on SDG 16 involves supporting human rights, promoting good governance, and fostering peaceful conflict resolution.

Solutions and how you can help

Realizing SDG 16 requires active engagement in promoting peace, justice, and strong institutions:

  • Advocate for human rights, equality, and access to justice for all individuals.
  • Support initiatives that promote transparency, accountability, and good governance.
  • Engage in conflict prevention and peacebuilding efforts at local and international levels.
  • Foster inclusive and participatory decision-making processes within communities.

By championing these initiatives, we can contribute to building peaceful and inclusive societies based on justice and strong institutions.
