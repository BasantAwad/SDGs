Information about SDG 7

SDG 7 aims to ensure access to affordable, reliable, sustainable, and modern energy for all. Despite progress, billions of people still lack access to electricity and clean cooking facilities. Achieving SDG 7 requires addressing various challenges such as:

  • Energy poverty and lack of access to electricity
  • Reliance on fossil fuels and unsustainable energy sources
  • Energy inefficiency and waste
  • Environmental impacts of energy production and consumption

These challenges need to be tackled through effective policies and interventions at local, national, and global levels.

Solutions and how you can help

There are several ways individuals can contribute to achieving SDG 7 and promoting affordable and clean energy:

  • Investing in renewable energy sources such as solar, wind, and hydroelectric power
  • Promoting energy efficiency and conservation
  • Expanding access to clean cooking facilities
  • Improving energy infrastructure and grid connectivity
  • Supporting policies and initiatives that encourage sustainable energy use

By taking these actions, individuals can contribute to ensuring everyone has access to affordable, reliable, and sustainable energy, which is essential for economic development and environmental sustainability.
