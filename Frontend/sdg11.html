Information about SDG 11

Sustainable Development Goal 11 (SDG 11) aims to create cities and communities that are safe, inclusive, resilient, and sustainable. This goal focuses on improving access to affordable housing, developing sustainable transport systems, preserving cultural and natural heritage, and enhancing disaster resilience in urban areas. Key aspects of SDG 11:

  • Provide affordable and safe housing for all.
  • Develop sustainable transport systems.
  • Preserve cultural and natural heritage.
  • Enhance resilience to disasters.
  • Promote inclusive and sustainable urbanization.

Solutions and how you can help

To support SDG 11, individuals and organizations can:

  • Advocate for sustainable urban planning and development
  • Promote energy-efficient and affordable housing options
  • Invest in public transport and walkable cities
  • Raise awareness about the importance of preserving cultural heritage
  • Implement waste reduction and recycling initiatives

Taking these actions can contribute to creating livable, resilient, and sustainable cities, helping achieve the targets of SDG 11 and improving the quality of life for urban populations.
