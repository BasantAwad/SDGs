Information about SDG 10

SDG 10 aims to reduce inequalities by addressing income disparities, promoting social inclusion, and ensuring equal opportunities for all. The goal focuses on lifting up marginalized groups and ensuring that economic growth benefits everyone, especially those most in need. Targets include increasing income growth for the poorest, promoting inclusion in all aspects of society, and adopting policies that reduce inequalities progressively. Solutions and how you can help

To achieve SDG 10, countries and organizations can take the following actions:

  • Implement Fair Policies: Advocate for fair taxation, equitable wages, and social protection programs.
  • Expand Education and Training: Provide better education and skills training, especially for marginalized groups.
  • Create Job Opportunities: Promote inclusive hiring practices and support entrepreneurship.
  • Raise Awareness: Educate and advocate for policies that promote social inclusion and equal opportunities.
  • Support Global Collaboration: Engage in international partnerships and initiatives that address global inequalities.

By implementing these actions, individuals and institutions can contribute to building a more inclusive and equitable world, ensuring that everyone has the chance to thrive and contribute to sustainable development. Together, we can work towards achieving SDG 10 by 2030 and creating a more just and prosperous future for all.
