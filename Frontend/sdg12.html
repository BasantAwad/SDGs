Information about SDG 12

Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 12 aims to ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns worldwide. This goal recognizes the need to decouple economic growth from resource use and environmental degradation. The targets of SDG 12 include:

  • Promoting efficient use of natural resources
  • Reducing waste generation
  • Encouraging sustainable practices in production and consumption

    • This involves addressing issues like food waste, excessive packaging, and unsustainable extraction of natural resources. Additionally, SDG 12 emphasizes the importance of educating individuals and industries about the benefits of sustainable practices and the long-term impacts of irresponsible consumption.

Solutions and how you can help

To achieve SDG 12, individuals and organizations can take several actions:

  • Adopting eco-friendly practices such as recycling, composting, and reducing single-use plastics.
  • Supporting companies and businesses that prioritize sustainable sourcing and production methods.
  • Advocating for policies that promote circular economy models and discourage overconsumption.
  • Raising awareness about the environmental impacts of various consumption habits through education and campaigns.

By embracing responsible consumption and production, we can reduce our ecological footprint and ensure a more sustainable future for generations to come.
