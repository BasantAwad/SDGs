Information about SDG 9

SDG 9 focuses on promoting sustainable industrialization, building resilient infrastructure, and fostering innovation. Key aspects of SDG 9 include:

  • Developing quality, reliable, and sustainable infrastructure, such as transportation, energy, and communication systems.
  • Supporting economic development and improving human well-being through enhanced infrastructure.
  • Encouraging inclusive and sustainable industrialization to create job opportunities and promote technological progress.
  • Recognizing the importance of innovation in driving economic growth and addressing global challenges.

Solutions and how you can help

There are several ways individuals can contribute to achieving SDG 9 :

  • Advocating for investments in infrastructure development and maintenance.
  • Supporting initiatives that promote innovation, technology transfer, and research and development.
  • Adopting sustainable consumption habits, such as reducing waste and supporting eco-friendly products.
  • Engaging in discussions, raising awareness, and participating in local and global efforts to enhance infrastructure and foster innovation.
  • Collaborating with stakeholders, including governments, businesses, and civil society, to advance sustainable industrial practices and infrastructure projects.
