Information about SDG 8

SDG 8 aims to promote sustained, inclusive, and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment, and decent work for all. Despite progress, many people around the world still lack access to decent work and face challenges such as:

  • Unemployment and underemployment
  • Informal employment and lack of social protection
  • Wage inequality and discrimination
  • Child labor and forced labor

These challenges need to be tackled through effective policies and interventions at local, national, and global levels.

Solutions and how you can help

There are several ways individuals can contribute to achieving SDG 8 and promoting decent work and economic growth:

  • Promoting inclusive economic growth and decent work opportunities for all
  • Ensuring equal pay for equal work and promoting fair labor practices
  • Protecting labor rights and combating all forms of exploitation
  • Investing in skills development and lifelong learning
  • Supporting small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) and entrepreneurship

By taking these actions, individuals can contribute to creating a more inclusive and sustainable economy that benefits everyone.
