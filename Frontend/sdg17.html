Information about SDG 17

SDG 17 emphasizes the importance of global partnerships and collaboration to achieve all the SDGs. This goal calls for:

  • Enhancing international cooperation.
  • Mobilizing resources for sustainable development.
  • Promoting technology transfer and capacity-building.

    • Taking action on SDG 17 involves fostering public-private partnerships, supporting development assistance to low-income countries, and strengthening global cooperation frameworks.

Solutions and how you can help

SDG 17 emphasizes the importance of collaboration and partnerships to achieve sustainable development:

  • Foster partnerships between governments, businesses, civil society organizations, and academia to mobilize resources and expertise.
  • Support development assistance programs that prioritize the needs of low-income countries and vulnerable populations.
  • Promote technology transfer and knowledge-sharing to address global challenges.
  • Raising awareness about the environmental impacts of various consumption habits through education and campaigns.

Engage in global advocacy campaigns to raise awareness about the SDGs and encourage collective action.
