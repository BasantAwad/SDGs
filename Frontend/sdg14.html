Information about SDG 14

SDG 14 aims to conserve and sustainably use the oceans, seas, and marine resources. This goal calls for:

  • Preventing marine pollution.
  • Protecting and restoring marine ecosystems.
  • Regulating fishing to ensure sustainable practices.

    • Achieving SDG 14 involves promoting marine protected areas, reducing plastic pollution, and supporting sustainable fisheries management.

Solutions and how you can help

Achieving SDG 14 requires collective efforts to protect marine ecosystems and promote sustainable practices:

  • Reduce plastic pollution by minimizing single-use plastics and supporting recycling initiatives.
  • Implement sustainable fishing practices and establish marine protected areas.
  • Support research and conservation efforts aimed at preserving marine biodiversity.
  • Raise awareness about the importance of oceans and sustainable ocean management.

By taking these steps, we can preserve marine life and ensure the health of our oceans for future generations.
