Information about SDG 3

SDG 3 aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all ages. Despite progress, millions of people still suffer from preventable diseases and lack access to essential healthcare services. Achieving SDG 3 requires addressing various challenges such as:

  • Lack of access to quality healthcare
  • High mortality rates for infants, children, and mothers
  • Non-communicable diseases such as heart disease and diabetes
  • Health emergencies and pandemics

These challenges need to be tackled through effective policies and interventions at local, national, and global levels.

Solutions and how you can help

There are several ways individuals can contribute to achieving SDG 3 and promoting good health and well-being:

  • Supporting universal healthcare coverage
  • Promoting healthy lifestyles and preventive healthcare
  • Investing in healthcare infrastructure and workforce
  • Addressing mental health and well-being
  • Responding to health emergencies and pandemics

By taking these actions, individuals can contribute to ensuring everyone has access to quality healthcare and promoting well-being.
