Information about SDG 2

SDG 2 aims to end hunger, achieve food security, improve nutrition, and promote sustainable agriculture. Despite progress, millions of people worldwide still suffer from hunger and malnutrition. Achieving SDG 2 requires addressing various challenges such as:

  • Food insecurity and unequal access to nutritious food
  • Declining agricultural productivity
  • Climate change impacts on agriculture
  • Rural poverty and lack of infrastructure

These challenges need to be tackled through effective policies and interventions at local, national, and global levels.

Solutions and how you can help

There are several ways individuals can contribute to achieving SDG 2 and eliminating hunger:

  • Supporting sustainable agriculture practices
  • Promoting food sovereignty and small-scale farming
  • Reducing food waste and loss
  • Investing in rural infrastructure and market access
  • Advocating for policies that prioritize food security and nutrition

By taking these actions, individuals can contribute to ensuring everyone has access to sufficient, safe, and nutritious food.
